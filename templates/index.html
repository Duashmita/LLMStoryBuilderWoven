How it Works

Welcome to Woven! This is an AI-powered emotional storytelling experience. You'll start a story by providing some basic details and your current/target emotions. The AI will write the beginning of the story and ask you a question. Your response will influence the story's direction and your character's personality arc. The goal is to guide the story towards your target emotion.

Your story personality traits (like Risk Taker, Optimism, etc.) will evolve based on your choices and responses. You can see these change in the sidebar as you play.

Data from your story (progress, moods, personality scores) is collected for research to improve AI narrative and emotional understanding. See the form notice for details on opting out.
